Chapter 1 – The Origins of Board Games

1.1 Play in Human History

Humans have played games for thousands of years, using them for entertainment, strategy practice, and social bonding. Some of the earliest games were simple contests of luck, skill, or both.

1.2 Evidence from Archaeology

Archaeologists have discovered carved stones, patterned boards, and ancient gaming pieces in burial sites and ruins. These artifacts reveal that games were a valued part of many cultures.
