Chapter 1 – What Is Color?

1.1 Defining Color

Color is the visual perception created by the way an object reflects, absorbs, or emits light. It is not a property of the object itself, but rather a result of how our eyes and brain interpret light waves.

1.2 Why Color Matters

  • Helps us identify objects and environments
  • Conveys mood and emotion
  • Acts as a form of non-verbal communication

1.3 Practice Exercise

  1. Look around your environment and list 5 colors you see.
  2. Describe what emotions or thoughts each color evokes for you.
